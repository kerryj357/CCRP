CCRP is a decentralized digital token designed to support Caribbean culture, art, and people, while enabling transparent peer-to-peer transfers within blockchain-based ecosystems.

About CCRP

CCRP operates as a utility-focused token deployed on a public blockchain. It is intended for use in environments that require verifiable transactions, open participation, and on-chain execution.

CCRP does not represent ownership, equity, profit-sharing, or voting rights in any company, protocol, or organization.

Cultural Manifesto

CCRP is rooted in Caribbean culture — a culture shaped by resilience, creativity, rhythm, and shared history. It represents a commitment to visibility, participation, and digital inclusion for Caribbean people and the global diaspora.

The Caribbean has long influenced the world through music, art, sport, language, and innovation. CCRP exists to acknowledge that contribution and to support cultural expression within modern, decentralized systems.

CCRP is not a promise of wealth or ownership. It is a utility token designed to enable participation, experimentation, and transparent interaction within blockchain-based ecosystems that value openness and accessibility.

This project stands for cultural respect, technological neutrality, and community-driven growth. CCRP is an evolving digital tool — shaped by use, creativity, and responsible participation — rather than speculation or centralized control.
